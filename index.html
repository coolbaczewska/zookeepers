See us in the dark ...

Copenhagen Zoo "After Dark" is an experience you will never forget. Come and see some of our nocturnal residents up close when they are awake!


You'll have the chance to see nighttime feeding of some of your favourite animals and also discover some new one's that are difficult to find in daylight hours.

Spørgsmål om dyr

Du er velkommen til at sende dine spørgsmål til zoo@zoo.dk. Alle skriftlige henvendelser angående dyrespørgsmål bliver besvaret inden for 14 dage. Du kan følge os på Facebook, hvor vi løbende besvarer spørgsmål. Prøv desuden at søge i zooleksikonet for at finde information om dyr.
