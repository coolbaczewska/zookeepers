See us in the dark ...

Copenhagen Zoo "After Dark" is an experience you will never forget. Come and visit our nocturnal residents on the last Friday of every month. See the lions roar, the wolves on their night patrol and the elephants enjoying their evening snacks.


You'll have the chance to experience the nighttime feeding of some of your favourite animals and maybe even discover some new one's that are difficult to spot during daylight hours. Check out our animal library for more information about the animals you will be able to experience throughout the evening.
